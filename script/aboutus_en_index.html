About NTUCA

NTU Climate Action Club (NTUCA) was founded in 2014 as the first university-based branch of the Taiwan Youth Climate Coalition (TWYCC). We are a student organization committed to raising awareness of climate change on campus and encouraging real action. We firmly believe that action is communication.

Our club logo—a pair of hands—features a gradient from the warm colors of global warming to the green of environmental protection, representing our commitment to bridging climate change and sustainable development.

Core Values

  • The Primacy of Sustainability Issues
  • Sustainability is the most critical issue of our time, affecting the very survival of human society. Without sustainability, other issues cannot even be discussed.
  • From Narrow to Broad Sustainability
  • Environmental sustainability should also integrate social, economic, and governance aspects (ESG), making sustainability a way of life for everyone.
  • Mindset and Values
  • We believe in “System change, not climate change.” Let's reform systems so that everyone becomes part of the solution. “Leave no one behind.” No one should be left behind.
